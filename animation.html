The Hunt of Tamos Guinivere

Canan, then twenty years old and after three years of formal training, was enlisted to assist footsoldiers along with two Crimson Order knights in searching for a man believed to be a catalyst for darkness, Tamos Guinivere. Following the search was a difficult and bloody fight, many men fell. Monsters and dark cultists mauled footsoldiers and even injured one of the Crimson knights. Once Tamos was found praying to darknss in the cellar of an old abandoned farm house, the remaining men knew what had to be done. But darkness has a way of manipulating minds. Most of th men were convinced to let the man live, the only ones immune to the effect were the Crimson Knights, but they still fought monsters outside. When he remaining foot soldiers turned away, Canan remained to plunged his blade in the heart of Tamos and end the outbreak. This gained the respect and the eye of the Crimson Order.
