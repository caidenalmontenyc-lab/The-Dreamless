Filnguard's Broken Scale

Canan, some years later, almost a man, lived dreamlessly an without purpose. He learned how to fight with and without weapons, and his hatred for monsters made him a natural at combat. He learned at first unofficially by different footsoldiers... until caught by a garrison commander. The commander decided to take Canan along and have him watch as monsters choked on blades of the soldiers. Canan studied the clashing of swords and every intricate move the warriors made in combat. Before Canan could recieve formal training, a major outbreak of darkness broke out in a few villages South of the capital.
